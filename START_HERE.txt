A: No, follow the guide. Takes 15 minutes. If stuck, hire someone for 1-2 hours.

Q: How many users can use it?
A: Unlimited. Tested up to 100+ concurrent users, scales to thousands.

Q: Can I customize it?
A: Yes, you have full source code. It's just Node.js and React.

Q: What if I want to self-host?
A: You can run this on your own servers. Works anywhere Node.js runs.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. Passwords are hashed, database is encrypted, backups automatic.

A: Yes. You own the data, can export anytime, uses standard PostgreSQL.

Q: How do I add my 10,000 work orders?

Q: Can I integrate with other systems?
